Cats are small carnivorous mammals they are known for their independent nature agility and grooming habits cats come in various breeds sizes and colors they have sharp retractable claws and keen senses particularly hearing and night vision cats are popular pets around the world and have been domesticated for thousands of years they are valued for their companionship and ability to control pests such as rodents despite their independent nature cats can form strong bonds with their owners and other animals in the household overall cats are beloved animals known for their grace and charm
